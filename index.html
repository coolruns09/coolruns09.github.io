PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: February 28, 2026

DJF Industries, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Launch Command Chrome Extension ("the Extension"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not run our own servers, and we do not actively collect, store, or sell your personal data.

  • Locally Stored Data: The Extension uses your browser's local and synced storage to save your notification preferences and temporarily cache rocket launch schedules. This data remains strictly on your device.
  • Subscription Data: If you upgrade to the Pro version, payments and authentication are handled entirely by a secure third-party service called ExtensionPay. ExtensionPay collects your email address and payment information to manage your subscription. We never have access to your credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Notifications: Your settings are used solely to trigger a desktop alert when a launch is approaching.
  • Subscription Status: The Extension communicates with ExtensionPay securely to verify if you have an active "Pro" subscription.

3. Third-Party Services

  • The Space Devs API: The Extension makes anonymous network requests to ll.thespacedevs.com to retrieve upcoming launch schedules. No personal user data is sent.
  • ExtensionPay: Used for subscription management. You can review their privacy practices at extensionpay.com.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties under any circumstances.
